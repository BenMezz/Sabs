About Me

  • Social Strategy & Management

    I understand how to utilize social media platforms to grow an audience based on client needs. I organize and manage content and strategy to create an online presence authentic to the brand's identity.

  • Graphics & Content Creation

    I craft visually appealing and engaging content aligned with artist or business objectives. I have graphic design and photo and video skills using Canva, Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, Procreate, Capcut, iMovie, and LogicX.

    I write engaging, original copy for social media posts tailored to the brand's voice.

  • Branding

    I design cohesive logos, color schemes and mood boards, and select fonts that perfectly complement a brand's identity.
